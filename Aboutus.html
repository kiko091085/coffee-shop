About us



Located in the heart of Cardiff, our coffee shop is a cozy retreat for coffee lovers and those looking to relax. We are conveniently situated at St Davids street, just a short walk from Central Library, making it easy to pop by for your daily caffeine fix. Whether you are commuting, exploring the area, or just enjoying a stroll, our welcoming atmosphere and delicious drinks are the perfect stop. We look forward to serving you soon.
